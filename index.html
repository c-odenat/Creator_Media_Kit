👨‍🍳 About the Creator

The Story Behind the Kitchen 🍳

C-O-D-E-M-A-N is Ghana's fastest-growing food and recipe creator on Snapchat, reaching over 13 million views and 750,000 unique viewers every 28 days. With an average watch time of 3.2 minutes — more than double the platform average — audiences don't just watch: they cook. 🍲 Rooted in authentic Ghanaian and West African culinary tradition, C-O-D-E-M-A-N bridges heritage recipes with modern kitchen techniques for a deeply engaged, action-taking audience. 65% of monthly views come from organic, non-follower discovery — meaning brand partnerships reach well beyond the existing audience. 📈
